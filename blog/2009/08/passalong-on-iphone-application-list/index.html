Adam: I just purchased the PassAlong v1.01 for my iPhone 3G. I have not been able to figure out how to use it for an email. I am not on twitter etc. The instructions say to find a contact and click on "Done". But, there is nothing that says "Done". Can you help? Thanks! Jim :)
Adam on 20 Aug 2009 at 3:21 pm
@James Smiley, The idea behind the app is to send snippets of information from one or more contacts. After choosing a contact, tap which information you would like to share (a phone number, e-mail address or anything else in their vCard). Once you have at least one piece of information selected, you will see a preview of the text to be copied and the 'Done' button. Tap the 'Done' button and then tap 'Send new e-mail message' which will launch the iPhone Mail application. Tap anywhere in the message body and then tap 'Paste' to insert the selected data. Thanks for supporting PassAlong! Adam
